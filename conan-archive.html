The land of Amarna needs farmers, crafters, mercenaries, and more! Our server offers an immersive RP experience crafted with precision by our admins who have taken a lot of time to help shape this server to be as fun and balanced as possible. With the help of many modifications, our server is packed full of new features, systems and tons of new items which have found their way into our economic system via vendors, but we wish to have players run their own stores and businesses. We have a strict set of rules which prevent things such as RDM, force RP and random raiding, our active and friendly staff members are there to ensure everyone has a fun and enjoyable time while still enforcing these rules.

Features:
  • PvP (RP Purposes)
  • Raids/Battles During Weekends (RP Purposes)
  • No Wipe or Thrall Decay
  • Standard Building Decay
  • 2x Harvest Rate
  • Thrall & Pet Hunger
  • Economy System (Pay, Tax, Trading)
  • NPC Cities (Shops, Warps, Quests)
  • Properties (Residential and Commercial)
  • Starting Roles
  • Kits (Building and Dailies)
  • Factions & Guilds
  • Pets & Thespians

  • Hosav's Custom UI (Minimap & Party System)
  • Stacksize Plus and Unlock Plus
  • Better Thralls (More Followers)
  • ExilesExtreme
  • Jewels of Stygia
  • Stygian Building Kit (More Building Pieces)

The server is hosted in East US but is also suitable for players in the EU, so come join us and help us build an amazing server!
