About Us

Welcome to Rusty Operations!

Our server brings a premium experience, offering a mixture of rebalanced gameplay, premium plugins and new maps. For US and UK/EU players!

Rusty Operations is a PVP modded experience that focuses on keeping the authentic feel from vanilla, where you can play knowing everything has been balanced and tested. We have many functionalities including: a real time voting system for 1 of our 7 maps, plenty of quality-of-life improvements to the core game, and many more. Here, you can expect a fully dedicated experience in both the United Kingdom and United States of America with staff is in both regions too. We have a very friendly and respectful community backed through Discord and we’ve that has been growing since 2019. We wipe bimonthly.

The communities wants and needs are always at great interest to us, as we like to shape the community and all of it's features around it's members. This means that we love feedback on anything, whether it's a game server, the Discord server or the content we put out, we want to know how you feel about the way the community is going. We are always more than happy to have more people join the family so if you are interested make sure to join the heart of our community, our Discord server. We'd love to welcome you!

Wipe Info:
Our server wipes bi-weekly. We do a map and blueprint wipe on the first Thursday of every month and a map wipe mid-month. This keeps us on schedule with the Rust monthly updates.

Add-on Events:

  • Raidable bases, 5 levels of difficulty.
  • PilotEject, scientist parachute from patrol heli.
  • PlaneCrash, shoot plane down with lock on rocket or has malfunction.
  • HijackableCH47, makes chinook hijackable.
  • Power Grid, steal power from power lines.
  • Convoys, challange with high rewards.
  • BetterNPCs, custom unique NPCs at certain monuments.
  • DefendableBases, setup defenses and fight hordes of zombies.

Shop

We really appreciate any amount of money donated as it will go towards helping the Rusty Operations community grow and offer our members a more improved experience. Running game servers can be very expensive, especially when running custom maps and premium plugins, and servers on some games can be even more expensive to run. Your donations will go towards the running costs of our game servers and the possibility of adding more premium features, maps or slots in the future. Having a large, lively, and friendly community has been our goal from the start and with your donations we can make this happen. You can make a one-time donation through PayPal or subscribe to our Patreon for some monthly benefits.
