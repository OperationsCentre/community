About Us

Welcome to the Operations Centre!

We're a friendly and helpful gaming community aimed at bringing people together, be it people that are looking for friends to play games with, or people just looking to belong to a group. We have a moderate set of rules that mean everyone can feel safe to socialise, play games, stream and attend events without feeling left out, bullied or discriminated against. Our attentive admins and moderators are there to help with any questions or problems and are very active within the community.

Here at the Operations Centre we offer many fun and social activities and opportunities, including:

  • Premium Conan Exiles Server
  • Game server events (wipe events, holiday events, etc.)
  • Catered Discord server (social, fun, sharing and streaming channels)
  • Discord server events (Jackbox, movie nights, etc.)
  • Fun and engaging community members
  • Friendly and helpful staff
  • Many social media channels (engagement with the community)
  • Frequent giveaways and freebie alerts

The communities wants and needs are always at great interest to us, as we like to shape the community and all of it's features around it's members. This means that we love feedback on anything, whether it's a game server, the Discord server or the content we put out, we want to know how you feel about the way the community is going. We are always more than happy to have more people join the family so if you are interested make sure to join the heart of our community, our Discord server. We'd love to welcome you!

Conan Server
Current Players Server Rank

Our Conan server offers a PvE experience on the Exiled Lands map with no wipes, a 2x harvest rate and a few quality-of-life mods aimed at creating more immersive gameplay. Our server is labelled as casual RP - this means that RP is not required or enforced but we try to encourage it. The server is host to many different features such as:

  • No wipe
  • Standard Building Decay
  • 2x Resource Harvest Rate
  • 2x Faster Crafting
  • No Thrall Decay
  • Longer Days, Shorter Nights
  • Economy System (Money, Payday, Taxes, Trades)
  • Market Cities (NPCs, Quests, Shops, Warps, Houses)
  • Kits (Starter, Building)
  • Pets & Thespians

The Operation Exiles server also has a few mods activated to add more features, items and an overall better experience. These mods can be downloaded from our Steam collection all at once and include the following:

  • Emberlight Mod (Painting Buildings, Horticulture, Animal Husbandry!)
  • Less Building Placement Restrictions Mod
  • IMMERSE RP Mod (New Decorations & Crafting Stations)
  • Fashionist Mod (Thrall Bench, Mannequins)
  • Shanis Stuff Mod (Colour/Resize Items, Tiny Workstations)
  • Accessory Wardrobe
  • Immersive Armor (More Armors)
  • Hosav's Custom UI Mod
  • Beyond Stations
  • Unlock Plus (with Pickup)
  • Stacksize Plus

The server is hosted in East US but is also suitable for players in the EU, so come join us and help us build an amazing server!

The Workshop Collection is required to play!

Rust Server

Rusty Operations is a PVP modded experience that focuses on keeping the authentic feel from vanilla, where you can play knowing everything has been balanced and tested. We have many functionalities including: a real time voting system for 1 of our 7 maps, plenty of quality-of-life improvements to the core game, and many more. Here, you can expect a fully dedicated experience in both the United Kingdom and United States of America with staff is in both regions too. We have a very friendly and respectful community backed through Discord and we’ve that has been growing since 2019. We host wipe events on the Rust server for holidays, and free giveaways! Overall, the memories you make here -- with other players -- will be memorable moments!

Donations

We really appreciate any amount of money donated as it will go towards helping the Operations Centre community grow and offer our members a more improved experience. Running game servers can be very expensive, especially when running custom maps and premium plugins, and servers on some games can be even more expensive to run. Your donations will go towards the running costs of our game servers and the possibility of adding more premium features, maps or slots in the future. Having a large, lively, and friendly community has been our goal from the start and with your donations we can make this happen. You can make a one-time donation through PayPal or subscribe to our Patreon for some monthly benefits.
